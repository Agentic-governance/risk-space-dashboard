## English

This service provides reference information only and does not guarantee safety. In case of emergency, call 110 (police) or 119 (fire/ambulance).

### Detailed Disclaimer

1. **Nature of Information**: Risk probability information provided by Risk Space MCP is based on statistical estimation and serves as reference material only. It does not guarantee safety at any specific location or time.

2. **Data Limitations**: Data used in this service has the following constraints:
   - Crime statistics are report-based and do not include unreported crimes (dark figure)
   - Some prefectures use synthetic data generated from national averages
   - Traffic accident data for 2021 uses interpolated estimates as NPA did not publish micro-level data
   - Suspicious person reports are collected in real-time but do not cover all incidents

3. **Emergency Response**: In case of crime, accident, or disaster emergencies, do NOT rely on this service. Immediately contact:
   - **Police**: 110
   - **Fire/Ambulance**: 119
   - **Coast Guard**: 118

4. **Liability**: The developers and operators assume no liability for damages arising from use of this service.

5. **Data Attribution**: This service uses public data from NPA, prefectural police, ITARDA, JMA, MLIT Kokudo Suuchi Joho, and OpenStreetMap contributors.

6. **Terms of Use**: Do not use information from this service as the sole basis for decision-making. Consult qualified professionals for formal safety assessments.
